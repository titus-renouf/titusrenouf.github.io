Business Intelligence Project for the Barreau de Paris

The goal of this project is to create a Business Intelligence tool for the deontology department of the lawyers order of Paris : Le Barreau de Paris.
This project is a tender made by the Barreau de Paris, indeed the goal is to convey an add of our technical capacity and not really a final and complete tool.
We had to query the initial database to create views that could be useable for creating interesting data. But this part is confidential and I can not publish it. So the explanation will only focus on the final product realized with Power BI.

Because we built data from existing deficient data, our stakes are not obviously the most effective.
The wish of the Barreau de Paris is to be able to compare the rate of complaints according to lawyers. Indeed we created a main page where you can find different repartition for a specific year chose by the user.
