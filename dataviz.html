Dataviz & Business Intelligence

The main challenge of data visualization is clearly the time saving in the search and analysis of information. The goal is to improve decision making. Business intelligence is the application of data vizualization on company sakes. Concretely, BI aims to transmit the right information to the right people.

A consulting mission realized for the lawyers order of Paris to improve the Business Intelligence service of the deontology department. We used Snowflakes, a SQL tool, to create queries to transform the data models, then we worked on Power BI to create the final product.
