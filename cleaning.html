Data Cleaning & Data Quality

Data Cleansing (Data Cleaning + Data Quality) involves identifying data errors and then changing, updating or removing data to correct them. Data cleansing improves data quality and helps provide more accurate, consistent and reliable information for decision-making in an organization. Data cleansing is a key part of the overall data management process and one of the core components of data preparation work that readies data sets for use in business intelligence (BI) and data science applications.
